## (REPORT) VIETNAM MUSIC MARKET RESEARCH — DECEMBER 2025  
### Strategic Patterns for Red Bull  
**Prepared by:** Senior Strategic Researcher  
**Research period:** December 2025  
**Client:** Red Bull Vietnam  

---

## PART 1: INTRODUCTION
December 2025 marks a special moment in Vietnam’s music market, as **Gen Z** officially becomes the dominant force on digital music charts. The year-end context—festive atmosphere, countdown season, and the boom of large-scale concerts—creates a vibrant music market where multiple trends coexist. [1][2][3][4]

This research is based on real-world data analysis from the **Top 20–30** standout songs across **ZingChart, Spotify Vietnam, YouTube Trending, and TikTok** in December 2025. Instead of analyzing each song individually, the report applies **Pattern Recognition** to cluster tracks sharing common characteristics into **four high-value Strategic Patterns**, and directly connects them to Red Bull’s brand DNA: **energy**, **breakthrough**, and **community connection**.

The ultimate goal is to deliver clear, measurable strategic recommendations to help Red Bull leverage music trends to build effective marketing campaigns and connect deeply with Vietnamese youth.

---

## PART 2: STRATEGIC PATTERNS

### PATTERN 1: THE RISE OF HEALING RAP — “GRIT AFTER CHALLENGES”
**Pattern description**  
December 2025 sees an explosion of **healing rap** and **storytelling rap**, replacing traditional “bragging/flex” rap. Songs in this cluster do not only showcase strong rap technique, but also carry deeper messages about maturity, learning from mistakes, and rediscovering oneself after hard challenges.

**Evidence from charts**  
1) **“It’s Gon’ Be Alright” — Negav ft. Văn Mai Hương**  
- Chart positions: **#3 ZingChart (Dec)**, **#8 Làn Sóng Xanh (Week 2 Dec)**, **Top 1 iTunes Vietnam**, **YouTube trending** [5][6][7][8]  
- Voting achievement: **2.6 million votes** at *Anh Trai Say Hi 2025*, far ahead of #2 by nearly **500,000 votes** [7][9]  
- Content: A self-narrative rap about “waking up” after mistakes, accepting the lesson, and continuing to write the future. The hook “It’s gon be alright” becomes a promise of peace after storms. [8][10]

2) **HIEUTHUHAI — a series of 2025 hits**  
- Chart position: Dominated Spotify Wrapped Vietnam 2025; most-listened male artist. [11][12]  
- Representative tracks: “Nước Mắt Cá Sấu”, “Exit Sign”, “Bật Nhạc Lên”. [12][13][14][15]  
- Characteristics: Rap/R&B with a more mature vocal tone and deeper lyrics about love, life, and growth.

3) **Low G**  
- Appeared at **Red Bull Húc Fest 2025**. [16][17]  
- Characteristics: Hanoi Old Quarter wordplay; blends rap with local cultural materials. [12]

4) **Buitruonglinh**  
- Achievement: **2.1 million votes**, ranked #2 only behind Negav at *Anh Trai Say Hi 2025*. [9][7]

**Why it stands out (decode)**  
After a period where Vietnamese rap focused heavily on “flex” (showing off wealth/achievements), the market is moving into a more mature stage with demand for deeper content. December is the year-end moment when people review their journey and look for encouragement to step into the new year. In particular, Negav’s return after a scandal in September 2025 with a message of “accepting mistakes and continuing forward” created strong resonance. [7][8]  
The report cites that **76% of Gen Z** associate new music trends with inspirational messages—helping explain why healing rap fits the moment. [18]

**Link to Red Bull (brand opportunity)**  
This pattern aligns strongly with Red Bull’s DNA:
- Red Bull’s “push forward” spirit matches the “It’s gon be alright” resilience theme.
- “Grit after failure” positions Red Bull as a companion for those who dare challenges.
- Combining **physical energy** (Red Bull) with **mental energy** (healing rap) creates a complete “energy narrative”.

---

### PATTERN 2: YEAR-END HEALING BALLADS — “REFLECTION BEFORE BREAKTHROUGH”
**Pattern description**  
Alongside high-energy music, December 2025 is dominated by sad ballads about breakups and emotional healing. These songs function less as “sinking into sadness” and more as **emotional release**, helping listeners reset before the new year.

**Evidence from charts**  
1) **“Thiệp Hồng Sai Tên” — Nguyễn Thành Đạt**  
- Chart positions: **#2 ZingChart Real-time (Dec)**, **#1 ZingChart New Music**. [20][5]  
- Viral: widely spread on TikTok and covered broadly. [21][22][23][24]  
- Theme: heartbreak when an ex marries someone else; the “wrong-name wedding invitation” becomes a shared symbol of regret.

2) **“Nhường Lại Nỗi Đau”**  
- Listed as **#1** in Zing’s “Top 10 best songs (Dec)”. [5]  
- Theme: accepting and letting go.

3) Other ballads in Zing Top 10 include:  
- “Người Đầu Tiên” (#4), “Nỡ Lòng Đồng Ý” (#5), “Đánh Mất Em” (#6), “Lần Sau” (#7), “Sau Tan Ca” (#8). [5]

4) Viral “healing ballad” YouTube playlists with very high view counts. [22][23][25][26]

**Why it stands out (decode)**  
Year-end is a time of closure—people acknowledge losses and unfinished emotions. For Gen Z facing pressure (study/work, relationships, social expectations), sad ballads become a safe emotional outlet. The report highlights a preference for authenticity over “forced happiness”. [18]

**Link to Red Bull (brand opportunity)**  
Not contradictory—complementary:
- Ballads represent the **mental recharge** phase, like recovery before performance.
- Red Bull can “own” the **calm-before-the-storm** moment: reflection that precedes an energetic leap.

---

### PATTERN 3: FESTIVAL ENERGY — EDM / VINAHOUSE REMIX “FLYING HIGH”
**Pattern description**  
December 2025 is peak festival season (Christmas, countdown, year-end events). Vinahouse/EDM remixes with strong bass and high BPM become the soundtrack for parties, clubs, and outdoor festivals—designed to hype crowds and generate collective energy.

**Evidence from charts/social**  
- NONSTOP Club 2025 Vinahouse Việt Mix compilations with large YouTube views; widely used by DJs. [27][28]  
- TikTok remix trend lists featuring remixes such as “Bắc Bling,” “Địa Đàng,” “Sự Nghiệp Chướng,” “Thương Thì Thôi”. [29]  
- TikTok remix trends for “Thiệp Hồng Sai Tên”, “Lệ Ngang Trời”, “Hẹn Hò Nhưng Không Yêu”, etc. [30][31]  
- Regularly updated TikTok hot-trend music playlists. [32][33]

**Why it stands out (decode)**  
December includes three major moments: Christmas (24–25/12), Countdown (31/12), and New Year’s Day (01/01). Youth gatherings peak. Vinahouse becomes highly demanded because:
- High BPM + heavy drops fit party environments  
- Short “drop segments” are highly viral on TikTok  
- Remixing familiar hits creates novelty and re-listens  

**Link to Red Bull (brand opportunity)**  
Natural fit:
- Red Bull = physical energy; Vinahouse = musical energy.
- “Wings” aligns with the idea of “flying high” in festival culture.
- Audience overlap is very strong (club/festival-goers are core targets).

### PATTERN 4: MODERNIZED FOLK — “ENERGY FROM ROOTS”
**Pattern description**  
A striking phenomenon in December 2025 is the rise of songs combining traditional Vietnamese folk elements with modern pop/EDM. These tracks reflect pride in cultural identity and attract Gen Z in a “return to roots” journey.

**Evidence from charts**  
1) **“Bắc Bling” — Hòa Minzy x NS Xuân Hinh x Tuấn Cry**  
- Appears in Google’s Top 10 most searched Vietnamese songs of 2025. [35][1]  
- ZingChart Week 12/2025: **#1**. [35]  
- Achievements include YouTube Trending performance and various music categories mentioned in sources. [1]  
- Cultural impact: described as contributing to place identity / tourism signaling for Bắc Ninh in coverage. [36]

2) **“Tái Sinh” — Tùng Dương**  
- In Top 10 most searched Vietnamese songs of 2025. [1][35]  
- Viral on TikTok; a live version reached **48 million views** (as cited in the report). [1]

3) Trend: bringing **đàn Nhị** (two-string fiddle) into contemporary music  
- Gen Z integrates đàn Nhị into pop/rap/EDM performances; examples include mixing Xẩm with EDM. [37]

**Why it stands out (decode)**  
After prolonged exposure to imported culture (K-pop, US-UK, global hip-hop), Vietnamese youth increasingly seek authenticity and identity. December also amplifies nostalgia and family connection, while setting the mood approaching Lunar New Year.

**Link to Red Bull (brand opportunity)**  
Theme: **“Energy from cultural roots.”**  
- Red Bull adapts globally; modern folk adapts tradition locally.
- Pride in identity → confidence to break through.
- Folk traditions connect strongly to festivals and community energy.

## CONCLUSION
Vietnam’s music market in December 2025 shows clear maturity through four parallel Strategic Patterns, reflecting diverse emotional states and Gen Z needs:
1. Healing Rap: grit after challenges (Negav, HIEUTHUHAI)  
2. Healing Ballads: reflection before breakthrough (“Thiệp Hồng Sai Tên”)  
3. Vinahouse/EDM: year-end festival energy (Nonstop Remix)  
4. Modern Folk: pride in roots (“Bắc Bling,” “Tái Sinh”)  

Across all patterns, the shared core is **energy**—whether mental energy (healing), physical energy (EDM), or cultural energy (folk)—all aligning closely with Red Bull’s DNA.

The five proposed strategic actions not only exploit each pattern individually but also build a comprehensive marketing ecosystem, helping Red Bull strengthen its position as “energy” in Gen Z’s mind—not only as an energy drink, but as a companion in every breakthrough journey. The report highlights **Q1/2026** as a “golden window” to act—both to launch the Tết campaign and to prepare for Húc Fest 2026.
